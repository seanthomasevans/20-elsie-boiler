Manifold & Connections

The manifold zone lives between the Rinnai bottom (24" AFF) and the floor. Six components, two circuits. All 3/4" CPVC with SharkBite push-to-connect.

Manifold Schematic

Six components, two circuits. Hot side (left): Rinnai output splits at a tee. One path goes through the TMV to DHW fixtures at 120°F. The other goes through the zone valve and circulation pump up to the air handler. Cold side (right): Municipal supply enters through an isolation valve, tees to the expansion tank, then into the Rinnai. Return (dashed): Water from the air handler returns through a check valve back to the cold supply upstream of the Rinnai.

TMV-1: Mixing Valve
Cash Acme HG110-HX
Blends 140°F down to 120°F for DHW. Downstream of heating tee.
ET-1: Expansion Tank
Watts PLT-5 (2.1 gal, potable)
On cold supply line. Pre-charge to match street pressure.
ZV-1: Zone Valve
Honeywell V8043E
Heating branch. End switch triggers pump relay when open.
CP-1: Circulation Pump
Grundfos UP 15-42
Downstream of zone valve. Creates flow to activate Rinnai.
CV-1: Check Valve
SharkBite 3/4" swing check
Heating return. Prevents backflow into fan coil when off.
BV 1-2: Isolation
SharkBite 3/4" full port
Cold inlet and DHW outlet. Service without full shutdown.

SharkBite Push-to-Connect Detail

Click to zoom

Every joint uses SharkBite push-to-connect on CPVC. The fitting has an O-ring seal, stainless steel teeth for grip, and a release collar for disconnection. Mark insertion depth on pipe, deburr, push until click. Use a tubing cutter for square cuts.

Assembly order: Hot outlet tee first (DHW + heating split). Then cold inlet with expansion tank tee. Then zone valve and pump on heating branch. Then check valve on return. Then isolation valves. Pressure test each joint at 40 PSI before proceeding.
