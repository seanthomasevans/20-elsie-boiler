Key Details

A
Rinnai RXP199iN mounted at 24" AFF on backing board. All connections exit from the bottom: gas (3/4" NPT), cold in (3/4" NPT), hot out (3/4" NPT), condensate (1/2" NPT). 199,000 BTU, 0.98 UEF.
B
First Company 36HX Air Handler mounted at 64-80" AFF. Horizontal fan coil with 1/2" copper supply/return connections. Receives potable hot water from the Rinnai for hydronic heating.
C
PVC concentric vent exits through left exterior wall. System 636 certified. 3" exhaust inside 5" intake pipe. Must maintain minimum clearances per Rinnai installation manual.
D
Manifold zone between Rinnai and floor. Contains TMV (Cash Acme HG110-HX), expansion tank (Watts PLT-5), isolation valves, and zone valve (Honeywell V8043E). All on 3/4" CPVC with SharkBite fittings.
E
Gas supply enters from left wall. Existing 3/4" black iron to unit. Drip leg with sediment trap at bottom of vertical run. Gas shutoff valve within 6' of unit.
Code note: Ontario Building Code requires combustion air supply for gas appliances in enclosed spaces. The RXP199iN uses sealed combustion (direct vent), drawing air through the concentric vent. No additional combustion air openings needed in the closet. TB-043 bulletin approves open-loop hydronic use with potable water.
