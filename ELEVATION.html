Front Elevation (looking at back wall)

Front elevation to scale (1" = 8px). Looking at the back wall. Both the Rinnai and the air handler mount on this wall. The Rinnai sits slightly left of center, allowing room for the expansion tank and heating supply/return pipes on the right side. All four Rinnai connections exit from the bottom. Gas supply enters from the left wall and includes a drip leg with sediment trap. PVC vent exits horizontally through the left exterior brick wall at mid-unit height.

Side Elevation (cross-section looking left)

Side cross-section showing the 32" closet depth. Left wall is exterior brick (with vent penetration). Back wall (right) is where both units mount. The Rinnai protrudes 11.45" from the back wall. The vent runs horizontally from the Rinnai through the exterior wall, sloping 1/4" per foot back toward the unit. Heating supply and return pipes run vertically between the Rinnai and the air handler above. Condensate drains down from the Rinnai to the floor drain.
