Front Elevation (looking at back wall)

Front elevation to scale (1" = 8px). Looking at the back wall. Both the Rinnai and the air handler mount on this wall. The Rinnai sits slightly left of center, allowing room for the expansion tank and heating supply/return pipes on the right side. All four Rinnai connections exit from the bottom. Gas supply enters from the left wall and includes a drip leg with sediment trap. Two 3" PVC vent pipes exit from the top of the Rinnai, run at 62" AFF under the air handler, and penetrate the left exterior wall.

Side Elevation (cross-section looking left)

Side cross-section showing the 32" closet depth. Left wall is exterior (with two vent penetrations). Back wall (right) is where both units mount. The Rinnai protrudes 11.45" from the back wall. Two 3" PVC vent pipes run from the top of the Rinnai at 62" AFF through the exterior wall. Exhaust against the back wall, intake 12" forward. Heating supply and return pipes run vertically between the Rinnai and the air handler above. Condensate drains down from the Rinnai to the floor drain.
