Notes

  • Left wall is the exterior wall. PVC vent exits here. Minimum 36″ from any openable window/door.
  • Rinnai mounted on back wall, left-biased. 4″ from exterior wall, 2″ side clearance met.
  • 12″ minimum clearance below unit to floor (24″ AFF mounting satisfies this).
  • Air handler occupies 64″–80″ AFF, edge-to-edge. Heat supply/return connect at its front face.
  • Expansion tank on right side at ~44″ AFF, teed off the cold supply line.
  • 120V outlet on left wall. Rinnai has a 5 ft factory cord, no extension cord permitted.
