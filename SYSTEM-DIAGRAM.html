P&ID NOTES

  • Open loop: Potable water flows through fan coil and back. No glycol. Legal per CSA B149.1 and Rinnai TB-043.
  • TMV: Rinnai fires at 140–160°F for heating. TMV blends hot + cold to deliver 120°F max to fixtures.
  • Zone valve: Opens on heat call, allowing hot water into heating loop. When closed, all output goes to DHW.
  • Circ pump: On return side. Pushes water through the loop. Controlled by zone valve end switch via relay.
  • Check valve: Prevents hot water backflow into cold supply when circ pump is off.
  • Built-in recirc pump: The RXP199iN recirc pump is for DHW recirculation only. You still need the external circ pump for heating.
  • Expansion tank: On heating loop. 2 gal, pre-charged to system pressure.
  • Oxygen-barrier PEX: Required for heating loop piping to prevent corrosion in fan coil.

ELECTRICAL NOTES

  • Nest "Boiler" mode: Must be set in Nest settings. Prevents Nest from controlling blower via G wire independently.
  • G wire: NOT connected. Blower controlled by aquastat, not thermostat.
  • Nest power: If only R and W wires exist, use Nest Power Connector or run a C wire from the 24V transformer.
  • Zone valve end switch: Single-pole. Directly shorts Rinnai S1/S2 (dry contact, no voltage needed). For circ pump, use DPDT relay: end switch energizes 24V coil, relay contacts switch 120V to circ pump.
  • Aquastat delays blower: Without it, blower starts immediately and blows cold air for 30–60 seconds. Aquastat waits until supply water ≥110°F.
  • MCC-91-2: Plugs into Rinnai PCB. Unlocks 160°F output. Required for hydronic performance (stock max is 140°F).
  • All 120V circuits GFCI protected per Ontario code for utility closet locations.
