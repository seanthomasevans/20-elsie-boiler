Open-loop design: This system uses potable water directly through the fan coil (no heat exchanger, no glycol). The Rinnai fires at 140°F for heating calls. The TMV blends hot output down to 120°F for DHW fixtures. The heating loop tees off before the TMV so the fan coil gets full-temperature water. Heating return re-enters cold side upstream of the Rinnai. Check valve CV-1 prevents backflow from supply into the return.
