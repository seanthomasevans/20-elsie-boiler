Architecture

  • Closed-loop CH: Treated water recirculates through fan coil. Separate from potable DHW.
  • Integrated pump: Grundfos UPS 15-78 built into unit. No external circ pump.
  • Flat plate HX: Internal heat exchanger isolates CH water from DHW.
  • DHW priority: Internal proportional valve auto-diverts capacity to DHW on demand.
  • Two expansion tanks: Potable (DHW, blue) + hydronic (CH, red). Separate circuits.
  • Fill valve: On CH return for charging closed loop.
