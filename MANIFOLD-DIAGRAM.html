Flow Logic

1Cold enters from main shutoff
2Through BV1, feeds expansion tank, enters Rinnai
3Cold also branches to TMV cold bypass
4Hot exits Rinnai through BV2
5Tee splits: right to TMV, down to heating
6Heating supply through ZV + BV3 to fan coil
7TMV mixes hot + cold to 120°F for house
8Return from fan coil through BV4 + check valve merges back to cold line
Installation notes:
• All four vertical drops from unit at 2″ spacing, plumb and parallel
• All ball valves at consistent heights per the 5-level staggered manifold
• Use CPVC for all water runs (rigid, easy to keep parallel)
• SharkBite push-to-connect at every joint (no soldering needed)
• P-clamp pipes to wall every 4 ft horizontal, 6 ft vertical
• Label every valve with adhesive tags (BV1, BV2, etc.)
• Run pipes parallel, never crossing unless unavoidable
• Pre-charge expansion tank to match incoming water pressure (50–60 PSI)
