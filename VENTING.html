Venting Detail

System 636 PVC concentric vent. 3" exhaust inside 5" air intake. Exits through the left exterior wall (brick). The RXP199iN is a sealed combustion (direct vent) Category IV appliance.

Type

Wall Penetration Cross-Section

Side view showing the vent running horizontally from the Rinnai through the exterior brick wall. The 3" exhaust pipe sits inside the 5" air intake pipe. Wall thimble with fire-stop caulk at the penetration. Exterior termination cap prevents rain/snow ingestion. Horizontal run slopes 1/4" per foot back toward the unit for condensate drainage.

Concentric Pipe End View

Click to zoom

Looking into the end of the concentric vent. The outer 5" pipe (cyan) draws combustion air inward. The inner 3" pipe (red) exhausts flue gases outward. Spacers hold the inner pipe centered. This design means all combustion air comes from outside. No room air is consumed. No additional ventilation openings needed in the closet.

Never mix vent materials. System 636 PVC throughout. Never connect to an existing B-vent or chimney. The RXP199iN is Category IV (positive pressure, condensing). Use only Rinnai-approved concentric termination caps. Condensate is acidic (pH 3-4), route to floor drain via 1/2" NPT at unit bottom.
